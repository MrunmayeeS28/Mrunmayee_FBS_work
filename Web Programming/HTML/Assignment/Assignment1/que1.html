Sports play a vital role in maintaining physical health, building teamwork, and fostering discipline. There are various types of sports enjoyed across the world, each with its own unique set of rules and cultural significance. Among them, Cricket is one of the most popular sports, especially in countries like India, England, and Australia. It involves two teams, a bat, and a ball, and is known for formats like Test matches, One Day Internationals, and T20s. Another widely loved sport is Hockey, often considered India’s national sport, which is played on a field with sticks and a puck or ball.
Football, or soccer, is the most followed sport globally and features two teams trying to score goals by getting the ball into the opponent’s net. Lastly, Kabaddi, a traditional Indian sport, combines strength, strategy, and quick reflexes, and has gained popularity through professional leagues and international tournaments. Each of these sports contributes to the rich diversity and excitement in the world of athletics.
